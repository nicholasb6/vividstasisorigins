# vivid/stasis Origins
**This requires the Origins mod, which you can get from [here](https://modrinth.com/mod/origins)!**

This is an Origins addon that adds new origins based on characters from the game vivid/stasis! Currently, there are 2 new origins added, those being Tsuki and Dawn.
## Powers
### Tsuki:
- The Worldkeeper's Scythe - Deal damage to entities around you.
- Into Another Cycle - Full heal, but gain Weakness and deal less damage with projectiles for an extended period of time.
- Dash - Dash in the direction you're looking.
- Teleport - Teleport to the block you're looking at.
- An Endless Duel - Deal more damage to people using the Dawn origin.

### Dawn:
- Freezeframe - Freeze entities around you for a short amount of time.
- Into Another Cycle - Full heal, but gain Weakness and deal less damage with projectiles for an extended period of time.
- Gun - Fire arrows in the direction you're looking.
- More ranged damage/Less melee damage - You deal more ranged damage, but less melee damage.
- An Endless Duel - Deal more damage to people using the Tsuki origin.
